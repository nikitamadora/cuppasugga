About the Team

Cuppa Sugga was devised by a trio of software engineering students during the COVID-19 quarantine. While questionably safe for elderly and immuno-compromised community members to leave their homes, Kris, Kita, and Beni decided to create an app that would allow anyone to put together a bag of necessities (or fun things!), and offer it to their community as a donation.
